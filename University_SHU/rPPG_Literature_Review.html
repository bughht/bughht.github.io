rPPG Methods (without Deep Learning)

BSS-Based Methods(PCA2/ICA3)

  • BSS: Blind source spearation

    • PCA: Principal Component Analysis
    • ICA: Independent Component Analysis

  • : factorized source signals consisting of pulse and noise
  • : demixing matrix (estimated by PCA or ICA)

Principle: Select the most periodic signal from as the pulse Shortage: Cannot deal with the cases of periodic motion.

limitations for estimation:

  • PCA: Covariance of RGB signals (ie., eigenvectors)
  • ICA: Assuming components in are statistically independent and non-gaussian, requires long enough for statistical measurement.
